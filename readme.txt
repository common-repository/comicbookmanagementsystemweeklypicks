== Description ==
Comic Book Management System Weekly Picks allows user to display seven comic book, picks of the week in an animated display. Designed for for use with blogs, reviews sites and stores.


Major features in Comic Book Management System include:

* Add your seven picks of the week for display on your website
* Your chosen books move in an animated display 
* The display is responsive to and adjusts to fit in any space you place it

== Installation ==

Upload and activate the plugin.
Choose your seven images and click "Add Weekly Picks"
To display your weekly picks add the shortcode [display_weekly_picks] to your site.
